Support Us

The Rule of Thirds Film Premiere Event will take place on November 12th, 2023, at Studio Max. For us to establish marketing materials and social media posts to include all our sponsors, the deadlines will be in advance of the event and will depend on the sponsorship tier that is chosen.

Sponsorship Tiers



Level One Sponsorship:

The deadline for sponsors who chose to contribute on the level one tier will be October 9th, 2023. This allows time for us to add business names to the credits as well as flyers.

Level Two Sponsorship:

The deadline for sponsors who chose to contribute on the level two tier will be October 13th, 2023. This allows us time to add logos to flyers.

Level Three Sponsorship:

The deadline for sponsors who chose to contribute to the level three tier will be October 27th, 2023.
