Lil Bau

Lil Bau is a talented artist and rapper known for his versatile melodies, dynamic flow, and fun selection of production. Born in New York and raised in Broward County, Lil Bau discovered his passion for music at an early age and began writing and performing their own songs while still in high school.

After taking time in attempt to hone his sound, Lil Bau released his first mixtape "Moon" in October of 2022, which quickly gained attention among his peers. Since then, Lil Bau has released a handful of singles along with EP, "36hrs*" as well as him starting to perform in live shows in 2023, bringing his raw energy and next-level engagement with the crowd.

With each new release, he continues to push his boundaries to better his sound while bringing a fun experience to his live shows.
